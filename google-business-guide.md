# ARYX Co. — Google Business Profile Guide

---

## Step 1: Create the Profile

Go to: **business.google.com** and sign in with the Gmail account you want linked to ARYX Co.

Click "Add your business" and enter the business name exactly as: **ARYX Co.**

When it asks for a category, pick **"Internet Company"** or **"Website Designer"** — whichever feels closer. You can change this later.

---

## Step 2: Business Type

Google will ask if you have a physical location customers can visit. Select **No** (since ARYX Co. is a fully online studio). This sets it up as a "Service Area Business" which is fine.

For service area, just add your country or city. It won't be shown publicly.

---

## Step 3: Contact Details

**Website:** Use your portfolio URL (whatever you end up hosting it on via GitHub Pages).

**Phone:** Optional. You can skip this if you only want contact via email.

**Email:** Your ARYX Co. email.

---

## Step 4: Verify the Business

Google will offer verification options. The fastest one right now is usually **video verification** or **phone call**. Sometimes they offer instant verification if the website is already indexed.

If they ask for a postcard, it takes 5-14 days. Just request it and wait.

Do not skip verification. An unverified profile shows a warning to anyone who finds it.

---

## Step 5: Fill Out the Profile Properly

Once verified, go to your profile dashboard and fill these in:

**Business Name:** ARYX Co.

**Category:** Internet Company (primary). You can add a secondary like "Digital Marketing Agency" or "Blog."

**Description (750 chars max):**

> ARYX Co. is an independent digital studio focused on building niche content platforms across education, health, beauty, and tech. Every project under ARYX Co. is built from scratch with a clear content strategy and a long-term growth mindset. Currently operating four live platforms: ARYX Guide, ARYX Lumina, ARYX Elevate, and Healthgrove.

**Website:** Your GitHub Pages portfolio URL.

**Hours:** Since it's online, set it to 24/7 or just skip hours entirely. GBP lets you hide hours for service businesses.

---

## Step 6: Add Photos

Google ranks profiles with photos higher. Add at minimum:

- A **logo** (ARYX Co. logo, square format, minimum 250x250px)
- A **cover photo** (1080x608px recommended) — could be a screenshot of your portfolio or any branded graphic
- 1-2 **additional photos** showing your websites or work

Keep filenames clean before uploading: `aryx-co-logo.png` not `IMG_20240312_093812.jpg`.

---

---

## Step 8: Services

Add services under the "Services" tab. Keep it simple:

- Niche Website Building
- Content Strategy
- SEO & Blogging
- Digital Monetization

---

## Step 9: Posts

GBP has a "Posts" feature. Think of it like a mini social feed on your Google listing. Post once every 2-4 weeks. A short update about a new article, a platform update, anything. It keeps the profile active and Google likes that.

---

## What to Avoid

Do not stuff keywords into your business name. "ARYX Co. SEO Digital Marketing Studio Pakistan" gets flagged and removed by Google. Just use "ARYX Co." and let the description and category do the work.

Do not use a fake address. If you don't have a physical location, don't add one. Getting caught with a fake address gets the profile suspended.

Do not buy reviews. One genuine review from a real person is worth more than 50 fake ones, and fake reviews can get the profile taken down permanently.

---

## After Setup

Once the profile is live and verified, search Google for "ARYX Co." — your Knowledge Panel (the info box on the right side of results) should appear within a few days.

To get the profile to show in local searches, you need real reviews. Reach out to anyone you have worked with and ask for an honest review. Even 3-5 genuine reviews make a big difference in how the listing ranks.
